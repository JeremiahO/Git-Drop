A few facts about the World we live in!



Earth, also called the world (and, less frequently, Gaia or, in Latin, Terra[26]), is the third planet from the Sun, the densest planet in the Solar System, the largest of the Solar System's four terrestrial planets, and the only astronomical object known to accommodate life.
